🎯 What is Visper AI?

Visper AI is built with one simple purpose: To give you a smart, helpful AI assistant right inside your Chrome browser sidebar. Instead of switching back and forth between different tabs or copying and pasting text manually, Visper AI works directly alongside your open web pages.

🛡️ Our 3 Core Promises

🔒
1. 100% Local Privacy

Your chat history, settings, and vector memories stay on your own device. We never track or sell your data.

⚡
2. Zero Complexity

No complicated setup. Select your preferred AI model or use offline Gemini Nano with 1-click simplicity.

🤝
3. Helpful Automation

Automates repetitive browser tasks like filling forms, finding size variants, and summarizing YouTube videos.

⚙️ Manifest V3 & Security Standards

Visper AI strictly follows Google Chrome's official Manifest V3 security standards. All code runs locally inside your browser package. There are no hidden tracking scripts or remote executable code. When you send a prompt, your request connects encrypted directly to your chosen AI provider (such as OpenAI, Anthropic, or DeepSeek) with zero middleman servers.
