🎯 Our Mission

Visper AI was created with a clear and dedicated purpose: To give you an autonomous, intelligent AI assistant right inside your Chrome browser sidebar. Instead of jumping between tabs or copying and pasting text manually, Visper AI works directly alongside your active web pages to handle shopping, research, and video summaries.

🛡️ Our 3 Core Pillars

🔒
1. Local Data Privacy

All chat history and document vector indexes stay stored locally on your device inside IndexedDB.

⚡
2. Zero Complexity

Simple 1-click operation. Plug in your preferred API key or use Chrome's built-in offline Gemini Nano.

🤖
3. Useful Automation

Automates form filling, size selection, coupon search, and bilingual YouTube video summaries.

⚙️ Manifest V3 & Chrome Security Standards

Visper AI complies 100% with Google Chrome's Manifest V3 security rules. All code is bundled inside the extension package. There are no remote code scripts, no hidden trackers, and no middleman servers. When you prompt the AI, your browser communicates via direct encrypted HTTPS with your designated AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, DeepSeek).
