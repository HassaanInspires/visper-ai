💡 Frequently Asked Questions

Is Visper AI free to use?

Yes! You can use Visper AI for free with Chrome's built-in offline Gemini Nano model, or plug in your own API key for DeepSeek, Gemini, Claude 3.5, or ChatGPT.

Is my browser data or password safe?

100% Yes. Visper AI never reads your passwords, credit cards, or private form data. All chat history and document vectors stay stored locally on your machine.

How do I summarize a YouTube video in English or Urdu?

Open any YouTube video and launch the Visper AI sidebar. Select 📺 YouTube Mode and ask Visper to "Summarize this video in Urdu" or "Give me key takeaways in English".
