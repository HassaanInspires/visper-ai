User Guide

Simple 4 steps to master Visper.

1

Install & Pin in Chrome

Click "Add to Chrome" on the Chrome Web Store page. Once installed, click the puzzle piece icon 🧩 at the top right of Chrome and pin 📌 Visper AI to your toolbar.

2

Select Your AI Model

Open the Visper sidebar. Click the top-right model selector to choose DeepSeek, Gemini, Claude 3.5, or ChatGPT. Add your API key in settings ⚙️ or select Offline Gemini Nano for free local completions.

3

Choose a Mode

Visper AI has specialized modes for your browser tasks:

  • ⚡ Auto Mode: Automatically detects the page context and picks the right tool.
  • 🛍️ Shopping Mode: Selects clothing sizes (Small/Medium/Large), tests coupons, and fills forms.
  • 🔍 Web Research: Searches multiple websites and organizes key points into simple comparison tables.
  • 📺 YouTube Mode: Summarizes long video lectures into clear English and Urdu bullet points.
4

Chat & Automate

Type your prompt into the bottom chat box! Ask Visper to "Summarize this video in Urdu", "Pick size Medium", or "Compare prices".
