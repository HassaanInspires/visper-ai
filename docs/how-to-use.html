How to Use Visper AI

Simple 4-Step Guide for Everyone

← Back to Home
1

Install and Pin Visper AI in Chrome

Click "Add to Chrome" on the Chrome Web Store page. Once installed, click the puzzle piece icon 🧩 at the top right of your browser and pin 📌 Visper AI to your toolbar for easy 1-click access.

2

Select Your Preferred AI Model

Open the Visper sidebar. Click on the model dropdown at the top right to choose your favorite AI provider (DeepSeek, Google Gemini, Claude 3.5, or ChatGPT). You can enter your API key in the extension settings gear ⚙️. If you want 100% offline free completions, select Local Gemini Nano!

3

Choose an Agent Mode or Use Auto Mode

Visper AI has special modes to help you with different web tasks:

  • ⚡ Auto Mode: Automatically detects what page you are viewing and picks the best tool.
  • 🛍️ Shopping Mode: Helps you pick shirt/shoe sizes, check cart prices, and fill forms.
  • 🔍 Web Research: Searches multiple websites and creates simple comparison tables.
  • 📺 YouTube Mode: Summarizes video lectures into clear English and Urdu bullet points.
4

Chat & Automate!

Type your question or request into the chat box at the bottom. You can ask Visper to "Summarize this page", "Find size Medium and add to cart", or "Compare the top features in a table". Sit back and watch Visper assist you!
