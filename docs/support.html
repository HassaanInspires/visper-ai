💡 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Visper AI free to use?

Yes! You can use Visper AI for free with Chrome's built-in offline Gemini Nano model, or plug in your own API key for DeepSeek, Gemini, Claude, or ChatGPT.

Is my browser history or password safe?

100% Yes. Visper AI never reads your passwords, bank details, or private form data. All extension data stays saved locally on your device.

How do I summarize a YouTube video?

Open any YouTube video and click the Visper AI sidebar button. Select 📺 YouTube Mode and ask Visper to "Summarize this video"!

What if I encounter an error or bug?

You can open a bug report directly on our GitHub Issue tracker, and our development team will assist you right away.
