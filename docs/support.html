💡 Frequently Asked Questions

Is Visper AI free to use?

Yes! You can use Visper AI for free with Chrome's built-in offline Gemini Nano model, or plug in your own API key for DeepSeek, Gemini, Claude 3.5, or ChatGPT.

Is my browser history or password safe?

100% Yes. Visper AI never reads your passwords, credit cards, or private form data. All extension chat history and document vectors remain saved locally on your device.

How do I summarize a YouTube video in English or Urdu?

Open any YouTube video and click the Visper AI sidebar icon. Select 📺 YouTube Mode and ask Visper to "Summarize this video in Urdu" or "Give me key takeaways in English".

What if I encounter an error or bug?

You can submit a bug report directly on our GitHub Issue tracker or send an email to our team, and we will assist you promptly.
