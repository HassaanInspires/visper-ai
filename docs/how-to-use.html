How to Use Visper AI

Easy Step-by-Step Guide for Everyone

← Back to Home
1

Install & Pin Visper AI in Chrome

Click "Add to Chrome" on the Chrome Web Store page. Once installed, click the puzzle piece icon 🧩 at the top right of your Chrome browser and pin 📌 Visper AI to your toolbar for quick 1-click access anytime.

2

Choose Your AI Model

Open the Visper sidebar. Click the model selector dropdown at the top right to choose your preferred AI (DeepSeek, Google Gemini, Claude 3.5, or ChatGPT). You can add your API key in settings ⚙️, or choose Offline Gemini Nano for free local completions.

3

Select an Agent Mode

Visper AI offers targeted modes for your daily web tasks:

  • ⚡ Auto Mode: Automatically detects the page you are on and chooses the right tools.
  • 🛍️ Shopping Mode: Selects clothing sizes (Small/Medium/Large), finds coupons, and fills out checkout forms.
  • 🔍 Web Research: Searches multiple websites and organizes key facts into simple comparison tables.
  • 📺 YouTube Mode: Summarizes long video lectures into clear English and Urdu bullet points.
4

Chat & Automate

Type your prompt into the bottom chat box! You can ask "Summarize this page", "Select size Medium", or "Find the best price". Visper AI will take care of the rest seamlessly.
